## 🛡️ SAFETY: What We Say vs. What We Do

### What We Say (Marketing/Positioning)

**Honest Messaging**:
- "Detects potential deception patterns in AI interactions"
- "Calculates value scores based on product attributes"
- "Provides recommendations based on detected signals"
- "Transparency: Shows detection methods and confidence levels"

**NOT Claiming**:
- ❌ "Proves deception" (we detect patterns, not prove)
- ❌ "Legal evidence" (we provide signals, not legal proof)
- ❌ "Guaranteed accuracy" (we show confidence levels)
- ❌ "Regulatory compliance" (we're a tool, not a regulator)

### What We Do (Actual Functionality)

**Detection**:
- Analyzes text for known deception patterns
- Calculates probability scores (0-1.0)
- Flags risk indicators
- Shows confidence levels

**Value Calculation**:
- Normalizes product attributes
- Applies mathematical formulas (WPM, TCO, GRACE)
- Calculates "bang for buck" ratios
- Flags manipulation attempts

**Decision Guidance**:
- Combines value + truth signals
- Provides recommendations (accept/caution/reject/verify_first)
- Shows risk flags
- Suggests next steps

**Transparency**:
- Shows detection methods
- Displays confidence scores
- Explains risk flags
- Provides source patterns
