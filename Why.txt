Rollback Journal Mode (Default)

Single writer at a time.
Readers blocked during writes.
File locks on main database file.
High risk of "database is locked" errors.

WAL Mode (Write-Ahead Logging)

Multiple readers concurrent with one writer.
Writes to separate -wal file.
Recommended for facts_registry ledger.

Busy Timeout

PRAGMA busy_timeout=5000 (5 seconds retry).
